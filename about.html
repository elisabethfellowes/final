About Me

Kate Grunzweig

MD Plastic Surgery Resident

I am Dr. Katherine Grunzweig, a Plastic Surgery Resident at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. A West Coast transplant to the Mid-West, where I am training in the art and science of Plastic Surgery. My interests vary from my own artistic pursuits to pursuing interesting and relevant research within our expansive field. I graduated from Yale University with distinction in History of Art and attended medical school at the University of Washington where I graduated with honors and subsequent induction into the medical honors society Alpha Omega Alpha.
